## Diagnosis (from response transcripts)

The organism produces **whole Wikipedia paragraphs** instead of synthesized answers:

- "Where is Paris?" → answers with BAFTA award paragraph
- "What happens to memory during sleep?" → answers with photosynthesis paragraph
- "Who invented the imaginary quantum pasta machine?" (should refuse) → answers with quantum mechanics paragraph
- "What are stars?" → answers with Nancy Kress biography

Broca (current generation) is fundamentally **regurgitating closest-overlap
training paragraphs**, not synthesizing short factual answers. This is the
primary bottleneck for `recall`, `generalization`, and `wikipedia`
categories. `reasoning` works because it uses the symbolic engine in
`reasoning.go`, bypassing Broca.

## Implications for the plan

1. **Broca 2.0 is blocker #1** — without a real autoregressive decoder
   conditioned on cortex state, no other improvement will raise the score on
   3 out of 4 categories.
2. **Refusal logic is broken** — failing to refuse `no confident response`
   cases is a calibration problem in Broca's selection of "best match".
3. **NeuroRadio sparsity is already excellent** —
   `TestNeuroRadioCortex_SparseSingle` reports 0.314% activation on 100k
   tiles. The 79% figure cited externally does not match current behaviour.
   Pista 2 (sparsity fix) is likely a no-op — to be confirmed by
   reproducing on `cortex-auto` runtime, not on the synthetic test.
4. **Reasoning at 100%** is a deterministic ceiling — it does not generalize
   beyond the symbolic patterns. Do not over-celebrate.

## Important observation — internal suites are saturated

`basic_recall`, `generalization` (small) and `no_echo` all score 100/100 on the
trained autonomous organism. These suites are **trivial** for the current
system and provide **no signal** for further optimisation. From now on, the two
meaningful suites are:

- **`comprehensive.jsonl`** — 32 mixed cases. Baseline = **33.5**
- **`hidden_benchmark.jsonl`** — 100 strict cases including Romanian + refusal. Baseline = **17.2**

The strict hidden benchmark is the honest measure. Anything that does not move
this number is decoration.

Reasoning on hidden_benchmark drops from 100% (on the easy suite) to **23.8%**.
This shows the symbolic regex engine in `reasoning.go` only solves the narrow
pattern set in the easy suite. The reasoning category on hidden_benchmark
contains cases the regex shim does not match, exposing the real ceiling.

## Pista 2 — NeuroRadio sparsity: no-op

The 79% activation claim cited externally does **not** match measured behaviour.
`TestNeuroRadioCortex_SparseSingle` reports **0.314% activation on 100k tiles**
(see test output post-merge). NeuroRadio is already at the desired sparsity in
the synthetic stress test. No action required for now. Re-verify on the actual
runtime path if a future change shows degradation.
